Data Analysis: 2020 Air Pollutant Emissions

Data Description


This dataset contains the air pollutant emission amounts by administrative district in South Korea for the year 2020. It includes the emission quantities of various pollutants such as Carbon Monoxide (CO), Nitrogen Oxides (NOx), Sulfur Oxides (SOx), Total Suspended Particulates (TSP), Particulate Matter (PM-10), Fine Particulate Matter (PM-2.5), Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC), Ammonia (NH3), and Black Carbon (BC) for each administrative region (province, city, and district). Additionally, it provides detailed information about the emission sources, including the major, sub-categories, and specific classifications of the sources.
